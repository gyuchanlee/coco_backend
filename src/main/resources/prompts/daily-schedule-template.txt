DAILY SCHEDULE PLANNING GUIDELINES:

Time Allocation:
- Morning (09:00-12:00): Attractions, Culture facilities, or Leports activities
- Lunch (12:00-13:30): Food/Restaurant
- Afternoon (13:30-17:00): Activities, Shopping, or additional Attractions
- Dinner (18:00-19:30): Food/Restaurant
- Night (21:00+): Accommodation (multi-day trips only)

Scheduling Best Practices:
- Start the day at 09:00 with an attraction or culture facility
- Plan 2-4 main activities per day (excluding meals and accommodation)
- Allow 1-3 hours per major attraction or activity
- End multi-day trips with accommodation around 21:00
- For single-day trips, end with dinner around 18:00-19:00

Place Selection:
- Prioritize places that match user themes
- Balance between popular attractions and hidden gems
- Group nearby places together to minimize travel time
- Ensure diversity in place types throughout the day
